I am a Research Affiliate at the **MIT Department of Economics**, a Research Associate at the **Laboratory for Economic Analysis and Design at MIT**, and a Collaborator at the **MIT Media Lab Digital Currency Initiative**.  

My primary research focus is at the intersection of **economics** and **cryptography**. I am currently engaged in the design of **blockchain protocols**, the analysis and design of **stablecoins** and other **digital assets**, and the design of **smart contract protocols** to improve the functioning of financial markets.  

Additionally, I work on the development of mechanisms to enhance the performance of **markets for environmental offsets**. I have authored two books on the **2008 global financial crisis**.
